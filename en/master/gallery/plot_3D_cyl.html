Cyl: Plot GridÂ¶

You can use mesh.plotGrid() to plot a z and theta slice through a 3D cyl mesh.

If you wish to plot only one slice, you can use mesh.plotGrid(slice='z') or mesh.plotGrid(slice='theta')
